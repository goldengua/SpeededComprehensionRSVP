In this task, you will read sentences one word at a time.

Please read the sentences carefully.

Each sentence is followed by a comprehension question.

The question always pertains to the immediate preceding sentence.

Press the key "f" to answer YES and key "j" to answer NO.

Some of the sentences will be presented very quickly, and you may feel that you don’t understand them.

This is fine, you can use your impression to answer the question.

Don’t “overanalyze” the sentences — try to read normally as much as possible.

When you are ready, press [SPACE] to begin.
